# Seeding Outreach Copy

Use these messages during the founder seeding sprint. Keep them short, route
specific, and personal.

## WhatsApp Group Post

`Wasel is opening a shared-route pilot for Amman <-> Zarqa.`

`If you travel this corridor often, you can search rides, offer seats, and move small packages on the same route.`

`We are keeping the first wave small so support stays fast. Reply with:`

- `Rider`
- `Driver`
- `Package sender`

`and we will onboard you directly.`

## Driver Outreach

`We are opening a small Wasel pilot on Amman <-> Zarqa and want a few trusted drivers in the first wave.`

`The goal is simple: visible route demand, quicker seat matching, and direct WhatsApp support while we build the corridor.`

`If you want early access, I can onboard you personally today.`

## Rider Outreach

`We are opening a founder pilot for shared rides on Amman <-> Zarqa.`

`If you already travel this route, I can help you search the corridor, save your route, and get first access when availability is live.`

`If you want in, reply with your usual day and time.`

## Package Sender Outreach

`Wasel is testing same-route package delivery on Amman <-> Zarqa.`

`That means small items can move with trusted corridor travel instead of a separate workflow.`

`If you send repeat packages on this route, we can include you in the first pilot and support the first requests manually.`

## Follow-Up After Search

`I saw you checked the Amman <-> Zarqa corridor.`

`If timing, trust, or price is blocking the booking, send me the issue and I will help directly. We are still in assisted launch mode.`

## Follow-Up After Ride Completion

`Thanks for using the Wasel pilot.`

`If the route worked well, send one short line about your experience and introduce one person who uses the same corridor.`

## Referral Ask

`We are growing this corridor carefully.`

`If you know one rider, driver, or sender who regularly uses Amman <-> Zarqa, send them to us now while founder support is still direct.`

## Objection Handling

### "Is this active yet?"

`Yes, but in controlled rollout. We are keeping the corridor small so support stays fast and the first users get real help.`

### "What if I do not find a match?"

`That is exactly why we are seeding one corridor first. We track route demand closely and follow up manually while the corridor gains density.`

### "Can I trust it?"

`That is the main focus of the first wave. We are onboarding people directly, keeping support visible, and building around known corridor behavior instead of broad anonymous traffic.`

### "Do you do packages too?"

`Yes, but only when the package stays close to the same travel corridor. We are starting with the routes that already show rider movement.`

## Testimonial Prompt

`Can you send one short sentence in this format?`

`I used Wasel on [route] and the best part was [reason].`
